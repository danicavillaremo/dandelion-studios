About Dandelion Studios

We’re a small studio with a soft spot for kind brands, cozy aesthetics, and visuals that feel like a gentle exhale. Dandelion Studios was born from a simple idea: design doesn’t have to be loud to be powerful. We help brands communicate their values with intentional visuals that resonate quietly, but stay in people’s minds.

What We Care About

Every project is guided by a few simple values that keep our work grounded, thoughtful, and kind.

Gentle, Not Generic

We love soft colors and calm visuals, but never at the cost of personality. Your brand should still feel unmistakably you.

Human-First Design

We design for real people: your audience, your team, and your future customers; not just a polished portfolio shot.

Calm, Clear Process

No jargon, no overwhelm. Just a transparent process with clear checkpoints, feedback rounds, and timelines.

How We Work

A gentle, structured process that lets ideas breathe while keeping the project moving.
